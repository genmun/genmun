ACCOMMODATION
Not sure where to stay during the conference? Fret not! Here are some recommendations by our team. If you have any inquiries, do not hesitate to contact us.
Kolej Ibu Zain, UKM
Fees: starting from RM98/night for 2 people
Distance from our campus: 3.4km
Kolej Ibu Zain, UKM provides luxurious and convenient hostel accommodation with first-rate amenities such as fully furnished air-conditioned rooms with water heaters and cafeterias, providing a tranquil atmosphere and modern lifestyle experience at an affordable price!
Learn More
D Gateway Perdana Hotel
Fees: starting from RM115/night for 2 people
Distance from our campus: 6.7km
D Gateway Perdana is strategically located in the heart of major townships such as Kajang, Semenyih, and Seri Kembangan, and is only a 15-minute drive from Pusat GENIUS@Pintar Negara. Its also part of the Bangi Gateway Shopping Centre, a buzzing pop-up fashion and dining destination that caters to all of your needs.
Learn More
Le'Venue Hotel
Fees: starting from RM198/night for 2 people
Distance from our campus: 11.8km
If you're looking for a hotel in Bangi that offers a lush tropical environment and a wonderful serene atmosphere, Le'Venue Hotel is definitely the perfect option for you!
Learn More
Tenera Hotel
Fees: starting from RM230/night for 2 people
Distance from our campus: 7.9km
Hotel Tenera Bandar Baru Bangi provides excellent service and superior facilities, all of which are guaranteed to make for an unforgettable stay.
Learn More
Bangi Resort Hotel
Fees: starting from RM528/night for 2 people
Distance from our campus: 7.7km
Bangi Resort Hotel is a picturesque 5-star conference resort built on the highest peak of Bandar Baru Bangi. It is surrounded by a 27-hole championship golf course, lush tropical greens, and beautiful vistas in a tranquil setting. Delegates are in for a relaxing night after a long day at the conference!
Learn More
Putrajaya Marriott Hotel
Fees: starting from RM390/night for 2 people
Distance from our campus: 18.4km
Hotel Marriott Putrajaya's breathtakingly beautiful architecture guarantees that you will have both an eye-pleasing and enjoyable experience. The IOI City Mall is also just a stone's throw away, so you can indulge yourselves! However, it's a tad bit far from where the conference will be held, so you will have to start your journey earlier.
